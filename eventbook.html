Introduction

Welcome to the eventbook.

In the eventbook we have written every single activity we can offer.
You can learn about each single activity before you experience it.
There are 12 activities and 2 categories for you to choose from so we've got all your needs available for you!

Sports

Welcome to the sports catergory.

With these activities you will mostly be moving your body to exercise.
You will experience some of your favorite sports, or maybe try something new.

Soccer

Welcome to soccer.

Soccer is the most popular sport in the world.
Soccer is a sport where you have to score with anywhere on your body besides hands.
two teams will be going agains eachother in a 11vs11 match.
We offer a smaller version of a stadium which in we have alot of training machines to help you train.
There are also a good bit of seats for spectators.

Basketball

Welcome to Basketball

Basketball is a sport where you play with 12 people in total.
Unlike other sports, you have to dribble with the ball to be able to move around the court.
If you fail to do so, you will recieve a penalty.
What we offer is a full basketball stadium for you to do whatever you want in.
This can be from training to an actual basketball tournament.

Bootcamp

Welcome to Bootcamp


At a bootcamp you will be working out outside with a group of people.
We have equipment and exercise activities for you to choose from.
You can also choose to mix camping with bootcamp,
wherein we will be camping outside and gathering our own food.

Rock climbing

Welcome to rock climbing


For rock climbing we have different areas where you can be in.
You can choose indoors or outdoors.
Outdoors is more for people that already have a bit of experience, we will be climbing actual mountains with safety gear obviously.
Indoors there are different colors which shows how skilled you are.
Easiest is green and hardest is black

Swimming

Welcome to Swimming


For swimming we offer a veriety of activities.
You can choose between free swimming and competition.
If you want to fully learn how to swim you would have to go and take official swimming classes.
With free swimming we will give you an entire swimming pool for you to enjoy with your friends, Life guards will be active just in case.
For competetion we will give you a private indoor swimming pool wherein everything is ready for you to do an official swimming competetion

Kickboxing

Welcome to Kickboxing


Kickboxing is like boxing but the twist is you can use your legs.
The key difference with kickboxing is that while boxing involves punches and blocks,
kickboxing involves kicks and punches.
For kickboxing we offer multiple variations of activities.
There will be 2 groups, 8 people in each group there will be 4 duo's.
Group 1 will be training and Groep 2 will be kickboxing

Music

Welcome to the Music category


In the music category we will be giving you alot of options that relate to music.
This goes from making music to dancing.
You can choose to make your own music, make remixes or make dance moves that fit into music.
Some people have talent for music, you wont know this talent unless you try it out.

DJ

Welcome to DJ


Have you ever wanted to try out being a DJ?
Have you ever wanted to make remixes of random sounds you hear?
Well then, here you can learn to be a DJ.
We give lessons to newbies, this goes from how the equipment works to how to make good sounding music.
For veterans we offer a room with all the DJ equipment you need for a party or disco.

Breakdance

Welcome to Breakdance


Breakdancing is a form of dancing which involes moving your entire body in a spin motion.
The type of music you will mostly hear while breakdancing is hip-hop.
We have a coach that will teach you how to breakdance.
If you already know how to breakdance then we also have a full stadium ready for you to perform

Karaoke

Welcome to Karaoke


Karaoke is already obvious, we have rooms with noise cancelling walls and in where you can sing your heart out
We also have instruments for people at the back to play along with your singing
We have 3 different type of rooms: small, medium and large
Small is for 1-4 people, medium is for 4-7 people and large is for 7-10 people.

Disco

Welcome to Disco

Choice 1 is for the space and choice 2 is for the equipment.
For a full on disco party you need those two things,
