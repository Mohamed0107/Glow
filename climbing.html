Welcome to climbing

How does the climbing work.
Climbing is the activity of using one's hands, feet, or any other part of the body to ascend a steep
topographical object. It is done for locomotion, recreation, and competition,
and within trades that rely on ascension; such as emergency rescue and military operations.
It is done indoors and out,
on natural and man-made structures.
